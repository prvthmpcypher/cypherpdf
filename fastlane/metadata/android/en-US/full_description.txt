CypherPDF is a PDF reader built for one reason: to open a PDF, let you read it, and get out of the way.

Most free PDF apps come with a sign-in screen, a home-screen icon nobody asked for, and a background process quietly reporting back to someone. CypherPDF doesn't have any of that. It has no launcher icon and won't show up in your app drawer — it only opens when you choose a PDF to open with it.

What it does:

* Continuous scrolling through pages, like a real document
* Pinch to zoom, and drag around while zoomed in without needing to zoom back out first
* Tap anywhere to hide the top bar and read without distractions
* Matches your system's light or dark theme automatically
* Jump straight to a page number instead of scrolling to find it
* Opens password-protected PDFs
* Share the open PDF back out to another app

What it doesn't do, on purpose:

* No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads
* No internet permission requested at all
* No editing, annotating, or form-filling — this reads PDFs, it doesn't edit them

Source code is fully open under the GPL-3.0 license.
